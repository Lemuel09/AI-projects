Programme Delivery Strategy

Ensuring 100% Venture Deliverable Submission

A structured, data-driven approach combining clear expectations, milestone tracking, participant support, and early risk intervention.

Clear Expectations

Provide clear deliverable requirements, templates, and examples during programme onboarding to ensure participants understand expectations from day one.

Milestone Tracking

Break the deliverable into structured milestones and track progress using a shared dashboard to ensure early visibility into participant progress.

Support Systems

Offer mentor office hours, peer accountability groups, and automated reminders to maintain engagement and momentum.

Risk Intervention

Identify participants falling behind through milestone tracking and provide targeted support to address blockers early.

Execution Process

Phase 1 — Alignment
Programme kickoff clarifies deliverables, timelines, and submission criteria.
Phase 2 — Milestone Monitoring
Progress tracking dashboards and regular check-ins ensure visibility across participant progress.
Phase 3 — Support & Accountability
Mentor sessions and peer groups provide guidance and reinforce commitment to deliverables.
Phase 4 — Final Submission
Structured reminders and final review checkpoints ensure timely deliverable completion.

Key Risks & Mitigation

Risk Mitigation Strategy
Unclear expectations Provide templates, examples, and onboarding guidance
Participants falling behind Use milestone dashboards and proactive outreach
Low engagement Introduce peer accountability groups and mentor support
Submission challenges Offer technical guidance and troubleshooting support
